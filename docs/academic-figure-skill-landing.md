## Owner Boundary

- `medical-figure-design` owns figure reasoning and candidate provenance.
- `medical-figure-composer` owns composition geometry and fit-policy checks.
- `medical-display-qc` owns deterministic refs-only artifact inspection.
- `medical-display-core` owns renderer source and regression fixtures.
- MAS or the consuming domain owner owns artifact mutation, visual-audit and
  owner receipts, typed blockers, current-package state, and publication truth.

Ordinary style or export warnings remain repair hints. Missing, unreadable, or
blank artifacts, invalid geometry, unsupported visible claims, or another hard
contract failure may stop the candidate path. Negative or equivocal scientific
results are valid evidence and must not fail a figure merely for lacking a
preferred signal.
